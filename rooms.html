Good to Know

The common questions.

How do I book a room?
Fill out the reservation form on the homepage or call 718-224-2434. We confirm within 2 hours. Walk-ins welcome any night, but weekends book up fast.
Can I bring my own cake or decorations?
Yes. Outside cake, balloons, and decorations are welcome for birthdays and bachelorette parties. Just let us know when you book so we can set up the room.
Is there a minimum or cover charge?
No cover charge. Room rental covers your time. Drinks and food are ordered separately, or you can choose one of our drink packages that bundle everything together.
What's the biggest room you have?
Our largest rooms fit up to 50 guests. For groups of 30+, call ahead — we can combine rooms or reserve a private floor for corporate events and big birthdays.
Do you have a song I can request?
Probably — the library has 100,000+ songs across every genre, era, and language. If something's missing, send us the title ahead of your booking and we'll do our best to queue it.
