Good to Know

The common questions.

How do I book a room?
Fill out the reservation form on the homepage or call 718-224-2434. We confirm within 2 hours. Walk-ins welcome any night, but weekends book up fast.
Is there a deposit?
Yes — a $40 deposit is required to reserve your room. We deduct it from your final bill when you check out. We will not have your card info on file at checkout — bring a card to settle.
What if I need to cancel?
Your $40 deposit becomes store credit. You can use it for another reservation or a future visit — it doesn't expire.
Is there a minimum?
1 hour minimum for all rooms. After the first hour, time is prorated — so you only pay for what you use. No cover charge. Drinks and food are ordered separately, or grab one of our drink packages.
Is there an age requirement?
All guests must be at least 21 years old after 8 PM on Fridays and Saturdays, unless accompanied by a parent or guardian. Before 8 PM and on other nights, all ages are welcome.
What do you hold during the stay?
We hold 1 ID and 1 credit card for the room during your stay. You'll get both back when you check out.
What's the biggest room you have?
VIP Room #1 is the largest karaoke room in NYC — fits 25 to 50 people at a flat $250/hr. VIP Room #6 fits 20–35 at $200/hr. Both are booked by the hour, not per person. Call 718-224-2434 for VIP availability.
Can I bring my own cake or decorations?
Yes. Outside cake, balloons, and decorations are welcome for birthdays and parties. Let us know when you book so we can set up the room.
Do you have a song I can request?
Probably — the library has 100,000+ songs across every genre, era, and language. If something's missing, request it and it may be added in the next monthly update.
